Our Current Projects

Replenishing the Seagrass

Manatees depend on seagrass as a main food source, but there has been a recent scarcity due to the weak roots of the current variety. Local colleges have partnered with us to engineer better and hardier grass for our adorable friends.

Learn More

Manatee Tracking Program

Manatees are known for their migratory behavior to avoid the cold waters in the winter. To track these manatees in their journey and know how best to help them, this project implements manatee-friendly trackers to aid scientists in their research.

Learn More

Manatee Fun Fact:

Manatees are more closely related to elephants than to any other marine animal!
