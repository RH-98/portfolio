Meet the Team

Founders:
Avery and Ryan Hilton

These two lovebirds started this organization just over 3 years ago based on their mutual love for these special creatures. They love to adventure when they get a chance, but you can often find them enjoying a good audiobook together or working on an art project.

Financial Manager: Manfred

Manfred has always had a knack for numbers and has been a part of the team from the very early stages of growth. He is not the most talkative, but conversations with "Manny" are riveting once you get to know him! Word to the wise, don't let him try your Skittles or you may not have many left.

Ambassador:
DJ Puddles

DJ Puddles is world-renowned for his funky music! He produces his boppin' tunes to raise more money for the projects we work on. Puddles always loves a good dance competition and his favorite move is the "Fin Spin!" By the way, his autograph comes free with any donation you make on this website!

Manatee Fun Fact:

Manatees suprisingly breath air and can hold their breathe for 20 minutes or more!
